Industries We Serve

Durga Analytics partners with organizations across diverse industries to unlock the value of data and AI. We combine strategic thinking with technical execution to deliver measurable impact for every sector we support.

Banking & Financial Services

We help banks and financial institutions modernize their data platforms, improve risk management, and deliver personalized customer experiences. Our solutions support regulatory compliance, fraud detection, and advanced analytics for strategic decision-making.

Energy Trading Risk Management

Our expertise in ETRM/CTRM platforms like Openlink Endur / Findur enables energy trading firms to optimize their operations, manage risk effectively, and comply with market regulations. We deliver consulting, upgrades, automation, and training services tailored to this complex industry.

Retail & Consumer Goods

We empower retailers and consumer goods companies with data-driven insights for supply chain optimization, personalized marketing, and enhanced customer experiences. Our solutions help you react to market changes faster and drive sustainable growth.

Social Impact & Rural Development

We partner with NGOs, government agencies, and social enterprises to use data for good. Our solutions support program monitoring, impact evaluation, and resource allocation to improve outcomes in education, health, rural development, and more.
