Modernizing Risk Reporting for a European Gas Trading Desk

Client

European Gas Trading Desk (Confidential)

Industry

Energy Trading

Challenge

The client’s legacy risk reporting system was unable to handle increasing trade volumes and evolving regulatory needs. Reports were delayed, siloed across teams, and lacked transparency. Traders and risk managers often worked from inconsistent data snapshots, delaying key decisions.

Objectives

  • Replace legacy Excel-based risk reporting with a modern, scalable platform.
  • Enable near real-time risk aggregation across gas trades, derivatives, and positions.
  • Improve auditability, transparency, and regulatory compliance.

Our Solution

Durga Analytics partnered with the gas trading desk to develop a modern, cloud-based risk reporting system.

Technology Stack:

  • Data Lakehouse: Databricks on Azure
  • Reporting: Power BI & custom dashboards
  • Data Engineering: PySpark, Delta Lake
  • Integration: APIs to ingest live trades from ETRM

Key Features:

  • Real-time ingestion and aggregation of trade, position, and market data.
  • Automated daily VaR and stress test reports.
  • Drill-down dashboards by portfolio, book, trader, and instrument.
  • Built-in data quality checks and anomaly alerts.

Security & Compliance:

  • Role-based access control aligned with internal risk policies.
  • Full audit trails on data transformations and report generation.

Business Impact

  • Reduced risk reporting turnaround time from 6 hours to under 30 minutes.
  • Enhanced visibility into P&L and exposure for risk managers and traders.
  • Improved confidence and compliance with internal and external audit standards.
