Prajesh Jha is the founder and CEO of Durga Analytics, a firm born out of his deep commitment to using data and AI for both enterprise excellence and social impact. With over 20 years of experience in financial services, consulting, and technology innovation, Prajesh has built and led high-performing teams across Fortune 500 firms, startups, and public sector programs.

Professional Background

  • Formerly with JPMorgan Chase, leading large-scale data modernization and analytics programs
  • Expert in data architecture, platform engineering, MDM, and regulatory analytics
  • Consulted for global banks, fintechs, and government institutions on data strategy
  • Founder of Gramin HealthGrid, a tech-driven initiative to bridge healthcare gaps in rural India

Areas of Expertise

  • Cloud-native data platforms: Databricks, Snowflake, GCP, AWS
  • Data governance, quality, lineage, and compliance
  • AI/ML implementation for BFSI, healthcare, and social development
  • Product management, pre-sales, and strategic consulting

Vision for Durga Analytics

Prajesh envisions Durga Analytics as a force that brings clarity and courage—values symbolized by Goddess Durga—to organizations navigating the complexities of modern data. His mission is to create a world where every decision is grounded in truth, powered by technology, and serves both business and human purpose.
