Social Impact & Rural Development

Using Data & AI for Inclusive Growth and Measurable Community Outcomes

Overview

Durga Analytics collaborates with government bodies, NGOs, and foundations to enable data-driven policymaking, monitor program effectiveness, and scale inclusive innovations in underserved regions.

Key Focus Areas

  • Program monitoring and evaluation with real-time dashboards
  • Targeting and outreach optimization using geospatial analytics
  • Livelihood enhancement and skill development analytics
  • Health, education, and gender equity impact measurement
  • Rural entrepreneurship, financial inclusion, and subsidy analytics

Approach

1. Understand: Align with social objectives and beneficiary segments
2. Build: Collect structured/unstructured and field-level data
3. Analyze: Identify gaps, risks, and improvement opportunities
4. Act: Inform decisions, scale impact, and share transparently

Outcomes Delivered

  • Improved rural health interventions through geospatial targeting
  • Increased program reach with mobile-enabled analytics tools
  • Reduction in dropout rates via predictive education modeling
  • Informed gender-focused interventions using sentiment analysis

Client Examples

  • Partnered with a state government for data-driven monitoring of tribal development schemes
  • Built impact dashboards for a rural skilling NGO enabling real-time reporting
  • Enabled financial inclusion analytics for a rural bank serving self-help groups
