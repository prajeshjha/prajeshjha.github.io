Case Study: Enterprise Data Management at a Global Bank

Led by Prajesh Jha | 2024

Overview

This case study presents the data management transformation of a global banking institution. Led by Prajesh Jha, the engagement focused on building scalable architecture, implementing modern governance frameworks, and enabling AI-ready platforms.

Challenges

  • Data silos across lines of business
  • Inconsistent metadata and lineage visibility
  • Manual reporting workflows
  • Need for federated data governance

Our Approach

1. Unified Architecture: Designed cross-domain data lakehouse combining structured and semi-structured assets
2. Data Governance: Implemented policy-based access, lineage, and cataloging using Informatica & Collibra
3. Automation: Enabled metadata ingestion, validation, and dashboarding via CI/CD pipelines
4. Cloud-Native: Deployed scalable workflows using Snowflake, Databricks, and Azure stack

Impact

  • 70% reduction in data access request cycle time
  • 40% improvement in data quality scores across domains
  • Operational reporting latency reduced by 60%
  • Platform now ready for AI/ML model integration
