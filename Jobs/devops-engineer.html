Job Description

We are seeking a skilled and security-focused DevOps Engineer / Infrastructure Engineer to design, implement, and maintain a production-grade, on-prem-ready infrastructure stack. This role focuses on supporting enterprise-grade open-source deployments for regulated industries such as finance, healthcare, and government.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and deploy secure, containerized infrastructure using Docker and Podman (rootless, daemonless)
  • Orchestrate microservices with Kubernetes (K8s) or OpenShift
  • Implement CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins or GitLab CI/CD
  • Configure persistent storage via NFS, HDFS, or Ceph with Rook
  • Set up centralized logging/monitoring using Prometheus + Grafana, ELK Stack, or Graylog
  • Integrate LDAP, AD, or SSO for authentication and RBAC
  • Configure NGINX or Traefik as a reverse proxy with SSL support
  • Collaborate with security/compliance teams to ensure audit-ready environments

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 5+ years experience with Docker, Podman, Kubernetes
  • Familiarity with OpenShift is a plus
  • CI/CD experience with Jenkins, GitLab
  • Knowledge of NFS, HDFS, Ceph
  • Hands-on with observability stacks: Prometheus, Grafana, ELK, Graylog
  • Integration experience with LDAP, AD, SSO
  • Expertise in NGINX or Traefik configuration
  • Scripting proficiency in Bash, Python, or Go
  • Comfort with air-gapped or isolated infra environments is desirable

Preferred Industry Experience

  • Telecom, Banking, Financial Services
  • Retail or Public Sector IT

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary & performance-based bonus
  • Opportunity to work on secure, self-hosted enterprise systems
  • Supportive team and leadership committed to engineering excellence
