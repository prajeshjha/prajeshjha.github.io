Overview

Durga Analytics delivers advanced ETRM (Energy Trading and Risk Management) services to support front-office trading, risk management, and regulatory compliance for power, gas, oil, and commodities trading firms.

Service Capabilities

  • Trade lifecycle automation and deal capture integration
  • Market and credit risk analytics, VaR and stress testing
  • Real-time position, P&L, and exposure reporting
  • ETRM platform configuration (e.g., Endur, Allegro, Openlink)
  • Custom analytics models for pricing, forecasting, and optimization

ETRM Service Framework

1. Assess: Evaluate current trading workflows, systems, and pain points
2. Design: Architect integrated and scalable ETRM solutions
3. Build & Enhance: Implement platforms, build analytics, and streamline operations
4. Operate & Monitor: Maintain systems, enable compliance, and optimize performance

Benefits

  • Improved risk visibility and faster decision cycles
  • Compliance with EMIR, REMIT, Dodd-Frank, and other energy regulations
  • Greater trading agility with real-time insights
  • Cost-effective modernization of legacy ETRM platforms

Client Examples

  • Modernized risk reporting system for a European gas trading desk
  • Built real-time position and exposure dashboards for a power utility
  • Implemented ETRM analytics for forecasting and pricing in a renewable energy firm
